    • Tokenization Pool

    After significant research and planning, we are at the final stages of realizing our latest product, Genesis Pool.

    Together with our partners Stokr.io, we are developing a tokenized music pool (fund) where artists, labels, songwriters, and catalog owners can allow fan to institutional level investment towards their music rights for the first time. 

    Funding

    Keen to see creativity flourish, we will be welcoming across the board genres, catalogs, and artists of various size and profile, as Genesis Pool, also aims to provide funding for new music projects whereby the content/asset owners can use their back-catalog/future royalties as collateral whilst retaining their copyright.
